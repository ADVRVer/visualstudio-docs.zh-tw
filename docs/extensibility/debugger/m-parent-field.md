支援欄位，如<xref:System.Threading.Tasks.Task.Parent%2A?displayProperty=fullName>屬性。</xref:System.Threading.Tasks.Task.Parent%2A?displayProperty=fullName>  
  
 **命名空間︰**<xref:System.Threading.Tasks?displayProperty=fullName></xref:System.Threading.Tasks?displayProperty=fullName>
